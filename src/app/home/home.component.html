About Us

Since 1963

Nandu’s is a part of the Nanda Group which was founded in the year 1963. We revolutionized the poultry industry through process innovation and advanced technology while staying true to our motto of ‘All Healthy All Fresh’

Farm to fork

To keep our promise of offering fresh meat, we operate our own feed mill, hatchery, breeding farm, processing unit, and retail stores.

Chicken Certified by Veterinarians

The chicken raised at our poultry farms are always monitored for any infections and certified by veterinarians before reaching your plate.

No Hormones, Steroids or Antibiotic Residue

Our chicken is 100% traceable without injecting steroids, hormones or antibiotic residue offering customers most hygienic and safe products.

Halal Certified
